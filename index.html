When a young boy vanishes, a small town uncovers a mystery involving secret experiments, terrifying supernatural forces and one strange little girl.

Did you know?

The show was originally called ‘Montauk’ and set on Long Island.

The Demogorgon is an animatronic, and it scared the little kids on set including the twins who played Holly Wheeler and Millie Bobby Brown’s little sister. Someone told the kids the monster came from Monstropolis in Monsters, Inc. to calm them down, the Duffers wrote in EW.
